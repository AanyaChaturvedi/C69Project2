Campus Life

School Church

was established in 1978

Our school church is on our beautiful campus! Our students visit the church every morning before we start our day at school. Our students are permitted to the church at any time of the day. On Sunday, our church is open to the public to pray. Our church is a big part of the Singapore International School.



School Library

was established in 2001

Our school library is a fun, place which not only is home to our hundreds of books, but also, the "Creativity Space" of the Singapore International School. Our students come to the library very often. Not just to check out and read books, but they like having group projects, in the area. It is also a great place to get work done!



School Auditorium

was established in 1983

The school auditorium is an area where we all gather for assemblies, announcements, and so much more! We have a top rated performing arts program, that focus on acting, singing, dancing, and playing instruments of the students' choice. Music is all about finding a passion, and sticking with it. Our directors ensure and help students find their musical passion through our program.
